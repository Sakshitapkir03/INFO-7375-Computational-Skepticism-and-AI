AI Use Disclosure

AI Tool Used

* ChatGPT (OpenAI)

How AI Was Used

ChatGPT was used to assist with structuring and drafting the required assignment artifacts. Specifically, it helped:

* Convert my resume into a structured resume.json format.
* Draft the initial profile.yml based on my stated career goals and constraints.
* Draft the initial gaps.md by organizing evidence, identifying potential skill gaps, and proposing evidence-based plans.
* Generate the initial RUN_LOG.md and this disclosure document.

Human Verification and Corrections

I manually reviewed every generated file before submission.

During the attestation process, I corrected AI-generated content where necessary, including:

* Reviewing extracted information to ensure that dates, experience, responsibilities, and skills remained supported by my resume.
* Ensuring visa-related information reflected verified facts or was explicitly marked as uncertain when it could not be confirmed.
* Removing unsupported assumptions and revising content so that it accurately represented my own experience and career goals.

What AI Could Not Do

AI could not determine facts that require access to my personal records or judgment. For example:

* It could not verify my exact OPT unemployment days from official SEVP records.
* It could not independently verify immigration-related details such as STEM eligibility without official documentation.
* It could not determine whether extracted information accurately reflected my own experience without my review and attestation.

Those decisions required my own verification before submission.
